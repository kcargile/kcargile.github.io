Kristopher Lee Cargile's first venture was a lottery, propped up in elementary school. It was a total bust when the principal confiscated his shoebox full of cash.

After spending a number of years consulting in tech and software development and cofounding and selling a successful healthcare startup, Kristopher has turned his professional focus to his true passion: running. His charge now is to build things that will foster the community that is so prevalent in running—one that has been instrumental in shaping his life.
